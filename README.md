About Every Child’s Journey Foundation
Legal name: Every Child’s Journey Foundation, Inc. • EIN: 39-4355181 • 501(c)(3)

Mission
Every Child’s Journey Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to ensuring that children with disabilities, developmental delays, learning differences, medical challenges, or financial hardship receive the support they need to thrive. We provide educational assistance, therapy resources, early intervention support, and family services that remove barriers to growth and opportunity.

Vision & Values
We envision a community where every child — regardless of ability or circumstance — has access to compassionate care and the learning opportunities needed to build a meaningful future. Our values include child-centered care, equity and inclusion, partnership, and transparency.
